We're eligibility-based, which means we're selective about who joins. This creates a focused learning environment with motivated peers. Most importantly, we treat education as a service, not just a transaction.

Our eligibility process involves a brief assessment of your academic background, goals, and commitment level. Click "Check Your Eligibility" or "Apply Now" to begin.
